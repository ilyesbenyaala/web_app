Price Ranges in Lisbon

Average Price Range: $$$$

Locations: Downtown Lisbon, Chiado, Avenida da Liberdade

Experience the epitome of luxury and gastronomy with high-end dining options in Lisbon. Indulge in exquisite dishes crafted with the finest ingredients and meticulous attention to detail. These establishments offer exceptional service and a memorable culinary experience.

Mid-Range Restaurants

Average Price Range: $$

Locations: Bairro Alto, Baixa, Cais do Sodré

Discover a diverse range of mid-range restaurants in Lisbon that offer a balance between quality and affordability. Enjoy delicious meals prepared with fresh ingredients, attentive service, and a pleasant dining atmosphere. These establishments cater to a wide range of tastes and preferences.

Budget-Friendly Eateries

Average Price Range: $

Locations: Mouraria, Alfama, Graça

Explore the vibrant culinary scene of budget-friendly eateries in Lisbon. These establishments offer affordable dining options without compromising on taste and quality. Enjoy delicious and satisfying meals that won't break the bank, perfect for travelers and those on a budget.
